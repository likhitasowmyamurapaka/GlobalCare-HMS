Symptoms

Based on the type of heart disease you may experience heart disease symptoms. Some of the common heart diseases are coronary artery diseases, heart valve diseases, and more.

Also, the symptoms may change based on your gender. For example, blood vessel diseases may cause chest pain more commonly in men than in women. Whereas women develop other signs and symptoms and chest pain like breathing difficulties, nausea, and extreme fatigue.

  • Heartburn
  • Nausea
  • Chest or Stomach Pain
  • Pain in the arm
  • Swelling in the Legs, Ankles, or Feet
  • Narrowed Blood Vessels
  • Fast Heartbeat
  • Fatigue
  • Fainting (syncope) or near fainting
  • Pale grey or blue skin colour (cyanosis)
  • Easily getting short of breath during exercise or activity
  • Irregular heartbeats that feel rapid, pounding or fluttering

With 2D/3D imaging, we obtain a crystal-clear view of structural abnormalities in the heart, enabling real-time assessment of cardiac structure and function.

At Global Care, our commitment to excellence ensures that you receive the best care from the top cardiologists in Hyderabad. This technology plays a crucial role in providing detailed anatomical assessments of cardiac pathology, with a special focus on valvular defects and cardiomyopathies. Trust us for precise diagnoses and state-of-the-art treatment procedures.
This technology helps cardiologists to find complications of the heart such as:

Intra Aortic Balloon Pump:

At the forefront of cardiac care, GlobalCare Hospital, recognized as the best cardiology hospital in Hyderabad, offers advanced procedures like the Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump (IABP). This therapeutic pump becomes crucial when the heart struggles to pump enough blood during or after a procedure.

During the IABP procedure, our expert cardiologists, known as the best cardiologists in Hyderabad, delicately insert a balloon into the aorta, a large artery leaving the heart. Through a small cut on the inside of the upper leg, a balloon pump catheter is then carefully placed into an artery.

Primarily designed for short-term purposes, this procedure supports patients until their heart condition improves or until they transition to other interventions. It is a vital therapeutic process addressing common heart complications, showcasing our commitment to comprehensive cardiac care at GlobalCare Hospital.

  • Unstable angina
  • Heart attack
  • Certain abnormal heart rhythms
  • Heart failure
  • Heart defects
