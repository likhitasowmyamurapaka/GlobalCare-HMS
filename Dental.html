Dental

Dentistry is a specialized medical branch focused on diagnosing, preventing, and treating diseases related to the teeth, gums, and mouth. Regular dental evaluations help prevent tooth decay, gum infections, and long-term oral complications. A Dentist expertly handles oral hygiene, cosmetic dentistry, dental surgeries, restorations, and emergency dental care.

Symptoms

Dental symptoms can vary based on the type of oral issue. Some may start mildly but can lead to serious complications if not treated early. Immediate dental consultation ensures better outcomes and prevents infection or permanent damage.

  • Toothache
  • Sensitivity to hot or cold food
  • Swollen or bleeding gums
  • Cavities or visible tooth decay
  • Bad breath (Halitosis)
  • Mouth ulcers that do not heal
  • Loose or shaking teeth
  • Jaw pain or clicking sounds
  • Swelling in gums or jaw
  • Broken or chipped tooth
  • Difficulty chewing food
  • Pus or infection near the tooth (abscess)
  • Dry mouth or burning sensation
