Dermatology

Dermatology is a medical specialty focused on conditions involving the skin, hair, and nails. Early detection and treatment are important to prevent complications and improve quality of life. A Dermatologist expertly diagnoses and treats skin infections, allergies, acne, hair fall, nail disorders, and chronic skin diseases.

As one of the top dermatology centers in Vijayawada, our care includes Clinical Dermatology, Cosmetic Dermatology, Dermato-Surgery, Skin Allergy Testing, Laser Treatments, Hair & Scalp Treatments, and management of chronic skin conditions like psoriasis, eczema, and dermatitis.

Symptoms

Skin-related symptoms vary depending on the condition. Some symptoms may appear mild initially but can worsen if ignored. Early consultation with a dermatologist ensures accurate diagnosis and timely treatment.

  • Redness or skin rashes
  • Itching or burning sensation
  • Acne or pimples
  • Dry, flaky, or scaly skin
  • Hair loss or thinning
  • Dark spots or pigmentation
  • Fungal infections
  • Allergic reactions and swelling
  • Skin discoloration
  • Nail cracking, thickening, or infection
  • Blisters or fluid-filled bumps
  • Patches of skin turning lighter or darker
  • Wounds that do not heal
