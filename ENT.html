ENT

ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) is a medical specialty focusing on disorders affecting the ear, nasal passages, sinus cavities, throat, voice box, and related structures of the head and neck. ENT specialists, also known as Otolaryngologists, diagnose and treat conditions ranging from infections, allergies, and voice issues to hearing loss and nasal obstructions.

At our ENT department, we offer comprehensive care including endoscopic sinus surgery, tonsil and adenoid surgery, ear surgeries, voice evaluation, allergy testing, and treatment for dizziness and balance disorders.

Symptoms

Depending on the type of ENT condition, symptoms may vary. Some ENT symptoms differ between adults and children.

  • Hearing Loss
  • Ear Pain or Discharge
  • Blocked Nose or Nasal Congestion
  • Breathing Difficulty
  • Frequent Sneezing
  • Headache or Facial Pain
  • Throat Pain
  • Voice Change or Hoarseness
  • Snoring or Sleep Apnea
  • Dizziness or Balance Issues
  • Tonsil Swelling
  • Sinus Infections
