Advanced Equipments

MRI Scanner

Advanced imaging system that uses magnetic fields to capture detailed internal body structures. Helps diagnose tumors, spine issues, and brain disorders.

CT Scanner

Produces cross-sectional 3D images of organs and tissues. Widely used for detecting fractures, internal bleeding, and heart-related issues.

Ultrasound Machine

Uses sound waves to create real-time images of internal organs. Commonly used for pregnancy scans and abdominal examinations.

ECG Machine

Monitors electrical activity of the heart. Helps diagnose arrhythmia, heart attacks, and rhythm abnormalities.

Ventilator

Supports patients who cannot breathe properly on their own. Delivers controlled airflow and maintains oxygen levels.

X-Ray Machine

Captures images of bones and internal organs using radiation. Essential for diagnosing fractures and chest-related issues.
