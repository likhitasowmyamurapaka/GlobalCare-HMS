About Gastroenterology Department

Gastroenterology focuses on diseases affecting the stomach, liver, pancreas, intestines, and overall digestive tract. Ignoring early symptoms may lead to severe conditions such as ulcers, liver disease, or gastrointestinal bleeding. Gastroenterologists specialize in diagnosing and treating such disorders effectively.

Symptoms

Gastro diseases can present symptoms based on the affected organ. Common issues include acidity, abdominal pain, liver disorders, indigestion, and bowel-related problems.

  • Acidity / Heartburn
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Abdominal Pain or Bloating
  • Indigestion
  • Constipation or Diarrhea
  • Loss of Appetite
  • Blood in Stool
  • Yellowing of Skin (Jaundice)
  • Severe Fatigue
  • Unexplained Weight Loss
  • Difficulty Swallowing
  • Stomach Cramps
