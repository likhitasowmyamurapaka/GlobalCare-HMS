Gynaecology

Gynaecology focuses on diagnosing and treating medical conditions related to the female reproductive system. Early detection helps prevent serious conditions like infertility, cysts, and hormonal disorders.

Our specialists provide expert care for uterine, ovarian, cervical, and hormonal issues with advanced medical and surgical techniques.

Symptoms

Common symptoms requiring gynaecological attention include:

  • Pelvic Pain
  • Heavy or Irregular Periods
  • Vaginal Discharge with Odor
  • Pain During Intercourse
  • Bleeding Between Periods
  • Severe Menstrual Cramps
  • Excessive Fatigue
  • Bloating or Lower Back Pain
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Frequent Urination
  • Difficulty Conceiving
