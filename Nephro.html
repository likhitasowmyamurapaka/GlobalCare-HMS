Nephrology

Nephrology is a medical specialty focused on diagnosing and treating kidney disorders. Kidneys play a vital role in filtering waste, balancing fluids, and maintaining overall body chemical stability. Ignoring kidney-related symptoms may lead to severe outcomes including kidney failure, high blood pressure, and electrolyte imbalance. A specialist in this field, known as a Nephrologist, manages diseases affecting kidney function and related systemic conditions.

As one of the leading nephrology centers in Vijayawada, we provide services such as Hemodialysis, Peritoneal Dialysis, Kidney Stone Management, Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) care, Acute Kidney Injury treatment, Electrolyte Disorder Management, Hypertension Treatment, Kidney Transplant Evaluation, and Renal Biopsy. Symptoms

Kidney disease symptoms may vary depending on the type and severity of the condition. Many kidney disorders develop silently, showing symptoms only in advanced stages. Early evaluation can prevent serious complications.

  • Swelling in the legs, ankles, or feet
  • Persistent fatigue or weakness
  • Pain in the lower back or sides (flank pain)
  • Changes in urination (frequency, color, or quantity)
  • Foamy or bubbly urine
  • Blood in urine
  • High blood pressure
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Loss of appetite
  • Shortness of breath
  • Muscle cramps
  • Swelling around the eyes
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Irregular heartbeat due to electrolyte imbalance
