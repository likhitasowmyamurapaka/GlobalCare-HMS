Neurology

Neurology is a medical specialty focused on diagnosing and treating disorders of the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles. Early consultation is important—ignoring symptoms may lead to severe complications such as stroke, seizures, or permanent nerve damage.

A specialist in this field, known as a Neurologist, evaluates and manages conditions like migraines, epilepsy, neuropathies, movement disorders, stroke, and more.Under the broad domain of Neuro Sciences, which includes Neurology, Neurosurgery, and Spine Surgery, Global Care Hospitals provides comprehensive services such as: Stroke Management & Thrombolysis,Neurosurgery (Brain & Spine), Minimally Invasive Spine Procedures,Epilepsy Management, Neurorehabilitation, Emergency Neurocritical Care.At the best neurology hospital in Vijayawada, our team ensures effective treatment for both common and complex neurological disorders.

Symptoms

Neurological symptoms vary depending on the part of the nervous system affected. Some common conditions include stroke, migraines, nerve disorders, epilepsy, and brain or spine issues. Symptoms also may differ based on age and gender. For example, women may experience more severe migraines, while men may show different early signs of stroke.

Also, the symptoms may change based on your gender. For example, blood vessel diseases may cause chest pain more commonly in men than in women. Whereas women develop other signs and symptoms and chest pain like breathing difficulties, nausea, and extreme fatigue.

  • Severe or persistent headaches
  • Numbness or tingling in arms/legs
  • Difficulty speaking
  • Sudden confusion
  • Loss of balance or coordination
  • Seizures
  • Muscle weakness
  • Fatigue
  • Vision disturbances (double/blurred vision)
  • Memory loss or behavioral changes
  • Tremors or involuntary movements
  • Dizziness or vertigo
  • Back pain radiating to legs (nerve compression)
  • Difficulty walking
  • Sudden facial drooping (possible stroke)

Procedures

EEG / Video EEG Monitoring (2D/3D Neuroimaging Equivalent)

At Global Care Hospitals, we utilize advanced EEG, Video EEG, MRI (2D/3D), and CT Neuroimaging, essential non-invasive diagnostic tools in neurology. These technologies help our expert neurologists obtain clear visual and electrical activity insights of the brain, allowing real-time evaluation of neurological functions.
This advanced neurodiagnostic setup plays a crucial role in identifying:

  • Epilepsy & seizure disorders
  • Structural brain abnormalities
  • Stroke-related damage
  • Neurodegenerative diseases
  • Brain tumors
  • Infections involving the central nervous system
  • Movement disorders
  • Headaches & migraine-related neurological changes

Intra Aortic Balloon Pump:

At the forefront of cardiac care, GlobalCare Hospital, recognized as the best cardiology hospital in Hyderabad, offers advanced procedures like the Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump (IABP). This therapeutic pump becomes crucial when the heart struggles to pump enough blood during or after a procedure.

During the IABP procedure, our expert cardiologists, known as the best cardiologists in Hyderabad, delicately insert a balloon into the aorta, a large artery leaving the heart. Through a small cut on the inside of the upper leg, a balloon pump catheter is then carefully placed into an artery.

Primarily designed for short-term purposes, this procedure supports patients until their heart condition improves or until they transition to other interventions. It is a vital therapeutic process addressing common heart complications, showcasing our commitment to comprehensive cardiac care at Global Care Hospital.

  • Unstable angina
  • Heart attack
  • Certain abnormal heart rhythms
  • Heart failure
  • Heart defects
