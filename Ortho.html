Symptoms

Common musculoskeletal symptoms that may require orthopaedic evaluation:

  • Persistent joint pain (hip, knee, shoulder, ankle)
  • Swelling or deformity after injury
  • Difficulty walking or bearing weight
  • Stiffness and decreased range of motion
  • Clicking, locking or giving-way of a joint
  • Chronic back or neck pain with nerve symptoms (numbness, tingling)
  • Symptoms of osteoarthritis or rheumatoid arthritis
  • Loss of function after trauma
