Pulmonology

Pulmonology is a medical specialty focused on the health of the respiratory system, including the lungs, airways, bronchial tubes, and chest wall. Pulmonologists diagnose and manage conditions such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), bronchitis, pneumonia, tuberculosis, lung cancer, and breathing disorders.

At our hospital, we offer comprehensive respiratory care including advanced imaging, bronchoscopy, pulmonary rehabilitation, sleep disorder evaluation, and treatment for both acute and chronic lung diseases.

Symptoms

Depending on the type of lung or respiratory condition, symptoms may vary and can range from mild to severe.

  • Shortness of Breath
  • Chronic Cough
  • Wheezing
  • Chest Pain
  • Frequent Lung Infections
  • Fatigue
  • Persistent Mucus Production
  • Snoring or Sleep Issues
  • Rapid Breathing
  • Breathing Difficulty During Activity
  • Blue Tint to Skin (Cyanosis)
  • Unexplained Weight Loss
