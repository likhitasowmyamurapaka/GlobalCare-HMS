Radiology

Radiology is a medical speciality that uses advanced imaging techniques to detect, diagnose, and guide treatment for a wide range of diseases. Radiologists interpret medical images such as X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, ultrasounds, and more to assist in accurate patient healthcare decisions.

Being one of the leading radiology departments in Vijayawada, our services include Digital X-Ray, Ultrasound & Doppler Studies, CT Scan, MRI Scan, Mammography, Fluoroscopy, Interventional Radiology, and Image-Guided Procedures. Our department provides precise imaging for early detection and treatment planning.

Symptoms

Radiology helps diagnose symptoms related to various organ systems. Imaging is often recommended when symptoms persist or when physical examination is not enough for accurate diagnosis.

  • Chronic or unexplained pain
  • Injuries needing internal evaluation
  • Breathing difficulties or chest discomfort
  • Swelling or lumps in any part of the body
  • Frequent headaches or dizziness
  • Abdominal pain or digestive issues
  • Bone fractures or joint pain
  • Numbness, tingling, or nerve symptoms
  • Irregular heartbeat or chest pressure
  • Pelvic pain or reproductive concerns
  • Sudden changes in vision or hearing
  • Fatigue with unclear cause
  • Trauma requiring internal scan
